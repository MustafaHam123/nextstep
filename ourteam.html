Why Nextstep?

Built by students who lived the struggle

We created Nextstep because we were just like you — trying to find real opportunities, overwhelmed, and frustrated that everything helpful was either scattered or too expensive.

Same premium AI. A fraction of the price.

Most platforms charge $20+ for the exact same AI models we use. We know most students (including us) can't afford that, so we priced Nextstep at $6.99 — the lowest we can make it while still improving the platform.

We faced the same problems, so we built the solution

We didn't build this as a company trying to sell you something. We built it as students who needed something that didn't exist — a place to find opportunities, get help fast, and grow.

Affordable, accessible, and student‑first

We're not trying to maximize profit. Our goal is to remove barriers, not create them. If we had to pay $20/month for academic help, we wouldn't have had access — so we made sure you don't have to either.
